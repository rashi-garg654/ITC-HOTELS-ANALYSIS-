# ITC Hotels Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes ITC Hotels performance data using Power BI to understand revenue trends, occupancy patterns, booking behavior, and cancellation impact. The interactive dashboard provides stakeholders with a single analytical view to monitor hotel performance and make data-driven decisions.

## Problem Statement
Hotels generate large volumes of booking and operational data. Without proper analysis it becomes difficult to track revenue performance, occupancy trends, and cancellation impact.

The dashboard helps stakeholders to:
- Monitor financial performance using revenue, ADR, RevPAR, and growth metrics
- Understand occupancy trends and seasonal behavior across hotels
- Identify high-performing and underperforming room categories
- Analyze lost revenue caused by booking cancellations

## Key Metrics
- ADR (Average Daily Rate) – Average revenue earned per sold room  
- RevPAR (Revenue per Available Room) – Revenue efficiency of available rooms  
- Occupancy Rate – Percentage of occupied rooms  
- ALOS (Average Length of Stay) – Average number of nights per booking  
- Booking Lead Time – Time between booking and stay date  

## Dashboard Insights

### Financial Performance
- Total revenue reached ₹1.71 Billion during the analysis period
- Elite and Premium room categories generate the highest revenue
- ADR is ₹14.92K and RevPAR is ₹7.35K indicating strong pricing and utilization

### Occupancy Analysis
- Overall occupancy rate is 43.5% across properties
- Week-over-week occupancy growth shows short-term spikes due to events or weekends
- Some properties show higher occupancy performance than others

### Room Category Insights
- Elite room category generates the highest revenue
- Average booking lead time is 3.71 days showing most bookings occur close to the stay date
- Average Length of Stay (ALOS) is 2.37 days indicating short-duration stays

### Cancellation & Lost Revenue
- Total lost revenue due to cancellations is ₹199.18M
- Elite and Premium rooms contribute the largest share of cancellation losses
- Cancellation patterns fluctuate due to seasonal booking behavior

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX

## Project Outcome
The dashboard provides insights into hotel revenue performance, occupancy trends, booking behavior, and cancellation impact, helping hotel management improve revenue optimization and operational decision-making.
